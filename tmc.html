All India Trinamool Congress (TMC)

A major regional party dominating West Bengal politics, TMC was founded by Mamata Banerjee after breaking from Congress. It champions Bengali identity, federalism, and social welfare schemes for the poor.

Founded: 1998 Ideology: Centre-left Symbol: Flowers & Grass HQ: Kolkata

Brief History

TMC was founded on 1 January 1998 by Mamata Banerjee after she broke away from the Indian National Congress. She ended 34 years of Left Front rule in West Bengal in 2011 and has been Chief Minister since. The party also won the 2016 and 2021 elections in West Bengal and plays a significant role in national opposition politics.

Ideology & Vision

TMC stands for centre-left politics with an emphasis on Bengali cultural identity and strong federalism. It opposes centralisation of power by the Centre. The party supports welfare programs for women, farmers, and students. It is strongly secular and advocates for state rights over Union interference.
